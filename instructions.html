Formatting

The Formatting has to follow this example in order for it to work properly.

    R1: 6 sc in magic ring (6)
    R2: BLO [inc] x6 (12)
    R3: BLO [sc, inc] x6 (18)
    R4: BLO sc, inc, [2 sc, inc] x5, sc (24)
    R5: BLO [3 sc, inc] x6 (30)
    R6: 2 sc, inc, [4 sc, inc] x5, 2 sc (36)
    R7-13: [Sc] x36 (36) 7 rounds
    R14: 2 sc, invdec, [4 sc, invdec] x5, 2 sc (30)
    R15: [3 sc, invdec] x6 (24)

The content before the number in brackets can be different. But, you must have the number of stitches per row for it to count properly. If you run into issues, you can email me @jacobatienza14@gmail.com
